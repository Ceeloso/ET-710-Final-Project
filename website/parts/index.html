JoJo's Bizarre Adventure!

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure tells the story of the Joestar family, a family whose various members discover they are destined to take down supernatural foes using unique powers that they possess. Its split into 8 unique parts, each following the story of one member of the Joestar family, who also has a name that can be abbreviated to the titular "JoJo".
